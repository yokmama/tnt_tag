ğŸ® Game Overview

TNT TAG is an explosive survival-style tag minigame for 20-25 players. Randomly selected "it" players carry TNT and pass it to others by hitting them. When time runs out, TNT holders explode and are eliminated.

ğŸ“‹ Basic Rules

  1. Round System: Fixed 6 rounds total
  2. It Selection: Random "it" players selected at round start
  3. TNT Transfer: TNT holders hit non-holders to pass the TNT
  4. Explosion: Only TNT holders explode when time runs out (no splash damage)
  5. No PVP: Player damage is disabled (TNT transfer only)
  6. Victory: Last player standing wins

ğŸ¯ Tips for Victory

ğŸ’¡ Strategy Tips

  • When you get TNT: Use your Speed II boost to aggressively chase other players
  • When escaping: Use obstacles to hide and maintain distance from TNT holders
  • Rounds 5-6: Everyone glows, so there's nowhere to hide. Predicting movement is key
  • Late Game: More TNT holders means constant vigilance
  • Arena Boundaries: Don't get cornered - always plan your escape routes

ğŸ® Game Flow

  1. Join: Use /tnttag join to join a game
  2. Wait: Wait for at least 20 players to join
  3. Countdown: 10 second countdown begins
  4. Round Start: Everyone teleports to arena center, TNT holders selected
  5. During Round: Pass the TNT! Watch the timer!
  6. Round End: TNT holders explode, survivors continue to next round
  7. Final Results: After all 6 rounds, the last survivor wins!
